The Band

When the menu for the Band are rendered selecting one of them will result in the other members becoming dull. Text will then appear for a biopic on each. Click on each pic of the band to generate a brief biopic below. In the biopic the text will inlcude a snippet of info that relates to the one of the Tour Sites being offered. The text will be slightly different colour so that noticable without the blue hyperlink. The link will take the user to the area on available tour packages. On Web it will have hover.
